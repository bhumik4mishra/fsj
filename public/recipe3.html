Spinach Potato (Aloo Palak) Recipe

Aloo palak is a classic North Indian dish that combines the comforting earthiness of potatoes (aloo) with the nutritious, slightly bitter notes of spinach (palak).

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp Oil
  • 1 tsp Cumin Seeds
  • 1 cup finely chopped Onion
  • 1/2 cup Tomato (diced)
  • 1 tsp Ginger Garlic Paste
  • 1/4 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1 tsp Red Chilli Powder
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1 1/2 tsp Coriander Powder
  • 4 cups Spinach Leaves (finely chopped)
  • 1 large Potato (boiled)
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil the potato. Peel and cut it into small cubes.
  2. Wash and finely chop the spinach leaves.
  3. Heat oil in a pan. Once hot, add cumin seeds and let them sizzle.
  4. Add chopped onion and cook until they turn light golden brown.
  5. Stir in the ginger garlic paste and mix well.
  6. Add coriander powder, garam masala, red chilli powder, and turmeric powder. Mix well and cook the masala for 2 minutes.
  7. Add diced tomatoes and mix well. Cook for another 2 minutes.
  8. Add chopped spinach leaves, mix, and cook for 3-4 minutes or until spinach wilts completely.
  9. Add the boiled potato cubes and salt to taste. Mix well.
  10. Cover and cook for another 3-4 minutes.
  11. Serve hot with roti or rice.
