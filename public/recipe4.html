Mango Lassi Recipe

A mango smoothie is a refreshing and creamy drink made with ripe, juicy mangoes blended with milk, yogurt, or ice. Itâ€™s the perfect way to beat the summer heat while enjoying the natural sweetness of mangoes. Packed with vitamins, fiber, and antioxidants, this smoothie is not only delicious but also a healthy breakfast or snack option. With its vibrant golden color and tropical flavor, a mango smoothie is loved by both kids and adults alike. Whether served chilled for a hot day or as a quick energy booster, this drink is a delightful treat you can make in minutes!

Ingredients

  • 3 medium-sized Mangoes
  • 4 tsp Sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1 cup Curd (yogurt)
  • Milk as needed (to adjust consistency)

Instructions

  1. Peel and chop the mangoes into pieces.
  2. Transfer the mango pieces into a blender jar.
  3. Add sugar and blend into a smooth puree.
  4. Add curd and a little milk to adjust the consistency.
  5. Blend everything again until smooth and creamy.
  6. Your mango lassi is ready.
  7. Serve chilled with a few ice cubes for extra refreshment.
